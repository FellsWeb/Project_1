About Us

N.O.W (Network of Outspokenness Worldwide) not only seeks to provide our users with awareness of the vast amount of social issues that impact our society daily, but also provide the tools necessary to get involved. Our team, "Coding-by-Parents", is comprised of parents seeking to become web developers, as well as help our community be part of impacting change at a social level.
